About

I’m a Master’s student in Artificial Intelligence at Sungkyunkwan University. My research focuses on GUI agents, multi-step reasoning, and self-play — building LLM-driven agents that can plan, act, and learn from interaction on real user interfaces.

At FCLab, I work on tool-using agents and evaluation for complex, multi-step tasks. Previously at OUTTA, I taught the OUTTA AI Bootcamp, contributed to a deep learning book project, and carried out AI alignment research.

I earned a B.S. in Intelligent Mechatronics Engineering from Sejong University. I enjoy open-source and collaboration - feel free to reach out.
